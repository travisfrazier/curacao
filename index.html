CURACAO SNORKELING

Curacao is one of the most breathtaking islands located in the Caribbean. It's home to a number of world-class beaches, beautiful scenery, and most importantly lots of places to snorkel. The islands aboundance of near shore reefs make it a top choice destination. Below you will find 5 of the best places to go.

Tugboat Beach

Tugboat is one of Curaçao’s most famous and popular dive sites. Named after the tugboat that was accidentally sunk years ago, this little wreck rests at just 5 m (17ft). Snorkelers will see that that there’s a lot more to the site, however, including a dramatic, pretty wall on one side, and a shallow reef to the other, home to a large variety of reef fish and cool creatures. The reef drops off much deeper for the more adventurous divers.
